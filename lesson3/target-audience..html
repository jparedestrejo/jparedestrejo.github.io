Target audience

Some businesses are highly influenced by customers' last-minute decisions, such as travel agencies, resorts and industries. So, businessmen can use the website to offer some promotions. Many restaurants and bars owners give price reduction on their menu based on the weather. At its heart the target audience of watchyourclima.com is for all young and adult persons.

Person One

  • Imaginary Name: Maria Quispe
  • Profession: Business Administration
  • Main Responsibilities: intern at IBM
  • Demographics: 20 years old, single, the youngest of two sisters, major in International Business
  • Work and Background: she likes to work upon pressure until achieving results in her field. She uses public transportation to go to her university, to her work, and to her home
  • Motivation for using the site: to know the weather conditions, she will use the site in her work, university and home, at whatever time
  • Devices: SmartPhone, laptop, tablet.

Person Two

  • Imaginary Name: Jhon Perez
  • Profession: geologist
  • Main Responsibilities: Head of excavations at the Marsa mine.
  • Demographics: 50 years old, married, He has three sons and two daughters
  • Work and Background: He study earth materials to find mineral for his company. He says that he has discovered a formula to predict earthquakes
  • Motivation for using the site: to know the historical data of the earth such as wind, temperature, precipitations, and atmospheric pressure. He thinks that with those data, he can predict earthquakes
  • Devices: SmartPhone, laptop, tablet, and PC.

Scenarios

  1. Little Jhon has to check the weather for the next week so that he can plan his route of excavation to certain place at the mining center. He wonders if watchyourclima site can tell him the weather conditions for the next week.
  2. Little Jhon wants to predict earthquakes from weather info and he needs some data to compute his magic formula. He wonders if watchyourclima site has historical data about the weather.
  3. Maria goes on vacation next month and doesn’t know whether to go to San Francisco or Miami. She wants to know if the site can help her make that decision.
  4. Maria wants to know for discounts. She knows that some restaurants give price reduction on their menu based on the weather. So, she wonders if the website has that information
  5. Maria is worried about the public transportation due to weather conditions. She wonders if the website can help her to avoid traffic-jams and to suggest alternative routes.
