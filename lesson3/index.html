Site name

After searching for many names, I finally get this one, watchyourclima.com. It will be a site that offers information about the weather all over the world.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of watchyourclima.com is to give reliable information about the weather in a city for decision making in your business, labor and tourism activities. Moreover, the website will provide weather predictions for the week or month. This main purpose will be fulfilled through detailed information on precipitation, clouds, temperature, wind, and atmospheric pressure.
