Signs You Have an Allergy

By Annie Hauser November 11, 2020

If you've never had allergies before, but are suffering through a runny nose, congestion or sinus pain, it could be that allergies are to blame. Seasonal allergies, including reactions to the ragweed and tree pollen that fills the air, can strike at any point in your life. "I think generally allergies have been misdiagnosed, or under-diagnosed, so people can definitely be walking around with an allergy and not know it," Stanley Fineman, M.D., of the Atlanta Allergy and Asthma Clinic, told weather.com. "What happens is that, sometimes, people can have symptoms of nasal congestion or breathing problems, and may not know that an allergic sensitivity is triggering it."
